    Get Involved


    SQSH, or the St. Louis Queer+ Support Helpline, offers free, confidential, and identity-affirming emotional support, resources, and referrals. For the St. Louis LGBTQIA+ community, By the community.

    Join the team!

    • Become a Peer Support Volunteer

      Are you a person who people come to when they need support? Ever wanted to hone your listening skills? Interested in improving the lives of LGBTQIA+ folx in St. Louis by connecting them to identity-affirming resources? Join the St. Louis Queer+ Support Helpline as a volunteer! We offer free meals, transportation (metro passes and a year-round carpool system), training in peer support skills, and assistance with career development (resume-building and letters of recommendation). You gain valuable helpline work experience, peer support skills, and crisis intervention techniques. You also join a supportive, empowering community of empathetic, dedicated LGBTQIA+ persons and allies!

      Folx who have personal experiences with or strong understandings of multiple marginalized identities (e.g. queer+ & trans folx of color) are especially encouraged to apply! To be considered for membership in the Helpline, please answer the following questions. We encourage you to apply as soon as you can as we accept applications on a rolling basis. Do limit each response to one paragraph. Please fill out either an online application OR print out the written application. Know someone who might be interested in joining? Share and nominate them using the nominate a friend application, and we'll reach out to them.

      Note: Applications for Summer 2020 are open from March 16 to April 17, 2020.

    • Join the SQSH Project Team

      Get involved in SQSH! We are a free, confidential, and identity-affirming peer support helpline service run by volunteers in the St. Louis LGBTQIA+ community, for the community. We work to serve and strengthen St. Louis - by providing an empathetic listening ear, by connecting callers safely and reliably to LGBTQIA-affirming resources, and by increasing the visibility of LGBTQIA+ concerns, needs, and resources in the St. Louis region.


      • Donate

      If you enjoy the services we provide to the Greater St. Louis community, please consider a donation. Any amount is welcome and equally appreciated. We take payments through Venmo, GoFundMe, Facebook Donations, Cash, and Personal Check (addressed to "St. Louis Queer+ Support Helpline").

      We deeply appreciate your donations, and we take them very seriously. We believe in accountability to those that put their trust and support in us, which is why we are providing the following information. To learn more about the financial and operational structure of our organization, you can read our Community Needs Assessment, our Project Summary, and our Full Project Proposal.

      If you would like more information or have a question, please contact us.

      Venmo Gofundme Facebook

      • Personal Checks

        Please write your check to "St. Louis Queer+ Support Helpline." Here are some options for getting your personal check to us:

        Email us to figure out a time and place to meet.
        We will take care to file your check in a secure place. In addition, we are working on offering paper receipts for your donation, upon request.

        Mail in your check.
        Since our office location is confidential, please email us for more details.

        Note: If you are interested in writing a check for more than $10,000, please Contact Us. We would love to chat with you and give you more information about SQSH. In the meantime, please visit Information for Potential Donors in Support Our Work for an overview of our financial and internal operations at SQSH.
